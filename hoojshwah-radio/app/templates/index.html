About Hoojshwah Radio

Hoojshwah Radio is a simulated-live broadcast of original, unrehearsed Hoojshwah music — basement transmissions, live-show fragments, long-form jams, strange suites, and moments that were never meant to happen twice.

Most of this music was captured live in The Spacement or pulled from real Hoojshwah performances, then arranged into a rolling signal for people who like their music raw, exploratory, funny, heavy, and alive.

Four released Hoojshwah albums are available to stream now. Search “Hoojshwah” wherever you listen, or visit hoojshwah.com for more.

Thanks for tuning in. Keep the signal weird.
